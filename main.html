Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up as a gig worker?
Simply download our app, create an account, verify your identity, and complete your profile with skills and experience. Once approved, you can start applying for gigs immediately.
What types of gigs are available on KrewsUp?
We offer a wide range of opportunities including event staffing, hospitality, retail, promotions, entertainment, technical support, and creative services. New categories are added regularly based on market demand.
How quickly will I get paid after completing a gig?
We process all payments within 24 hours of gig completion and confirmation. Funds are directly transferred to your linked bank account or digital wallet, making the process fast and hassle-free.
Is there a fee for using KrewsUp?
Creating a basic profile is free. We charge a small service fee (5-10% depending on the gig type) only when a gig is successfully completed. Hosts pay a listing fee based on the size and complexity of their requirements.
How does KrewsUp ensure quality and reliability?
We have a comprehensive verification process including ID verification, background checks for certain roles, skills assessment, and a robust review system. Poor performance is monitored and addressed promptly to maintain platform quality.
